    Description

    Use the mouse to place and erase blocks to reach the goal!
    The player can move and jump.
    Mouse click to place blocks.
    Blocks disappear when 4 blocks are aligned vertically or horizontally.
    Placing and erasing blocks will allow you to go to the places you could not go!

    Controls

    < Menu >

    * Mouse Cursor or Arrow Keys or WASD - Select
    * Mouse Click or [Space] or [Enter] or [Z] - Confirm
    * [Escape] or [Backspace] or [X] - Back

    < Game >

    * Arrow Keys or WASD - Move Player, Jump Player
    * Mouse Click - Place a block
    * [P] or [Space] - Pause
    * [R] - Retry
